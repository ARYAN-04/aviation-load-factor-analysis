Metrics-

ASK: Available Seat Kilometres (Total passenger capacity available)
RPK: Revenue Passenger Kilometres (Actual passenger demand filled)
PLF: Passenger Load Factor (%) (Calculated as RPK / ASK)
ATK: Available Tonne Kilometres (Total weight capacity available for payload)
WLF: Weight Load Factor (%) (Calculated as Utilized capacity / ATK)
Date: Financial Year format (MM/YY, April to March)
